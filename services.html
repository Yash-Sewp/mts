Services & Fleet

Our fleet consist of 1-8 ton trucks, superlink trucks, light delivery vehicles, flat deck and curtain side trailers to drop sides trucks, panel van trucks, and curtain side trucks . We have implemented monitoring and communication systems that enable us to keep in constant contact with every single truck and driver.
